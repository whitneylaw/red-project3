Welcome to a wine club with a difference

THE Club @ Crush Pad

While other wine clubs offer a set "mystery" selection throughout the year, THE Club @ Crush Pad lets members choose which wines they want, when they want them. All we ask is that you commit to purchasing two full cases within a year, for which we give you 20% off our wines.


There are NO membership fees, and you choose the wines you want from THE Club offerings. Your commitment is to purchase two cases (24 bottles) per year. Club members must be aged 19 years or older.


As a club member you receive other exclusive benefits including advance notice on events, special "end of lot" offerings, discounts on all wines in our portfolio, member-only preferential shipping rates, new updates, tasting notes, food pairing suggestions, and more!
